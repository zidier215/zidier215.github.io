最新入境政策

Latest Entry Policy

签证要求

Visa Requirements

  • 大多数国家公民需要提前申请中国签证。部分国家可享受72小时或144小时过境免签政策。
  • Citizens of most countries need to apply for a Chinese visa in advance. Citizens of some countries can enjoy 72-hour or 144-hour visa-free transit policies.
  • 2023年起，中国对多个国家实施单方面免签政策，停留期最长15天。
  • Since 2023, China has implemented a unilateral visa-free policy for several countries, with a maximum stay of 15 days.
  • 建议出行前查看中国驻当地使领馆最新公告，确认签证要求。
  • It is recommended to check the latest announcements from Chinese embassies and consulates in your country before traveling to confirm visa requirements.

入境须知

Entry Information

  • 目前入境中国已无需提供核酸检测阴性证明和疫苗接种证明。
  • Currently, negative nucleic acid test certificates and vaccination certificates are no longer required for entry into China.
  • 建议提前下载并注册"海关旅客指尖服务"小程序，完成健康申报。
  • It is recommended to download and register the "Customs Passenger Fingertip Service" applet in advance to complete health declaration.
  • 随身携带有效护照和签证/居留许可等证件原件。
  • Carry original documents such as valid passport and visa/residence permit with you.

注：入境政策可能会根据实际情况调整，请在出行前通过官方渠道确认最新信息。

Note: Entry policies may be adjusted according to actual conditions. Please confirm the latest information through official channels before traveling.

Beijing's Top Attractions

故宫博物院

Forbidden City

中国明清两代皇家宫殿，世界上现存规模最大、保存最为完整的木质结构古建筑之一

Imperial palace of the Ming and Qing dynasties, one of the largest and best-preserved wooden structures in the world

市中心 需预约
City Center Reservation required

八达岭长城

Badaling Great Wall

明长城中保存最好的一段，世界文化遗产，是中外游客游览长城的首选之地

Best-preserved section of the Ming Dynasty Great Wall, a World Heritage Site and top choice for visitors

西北郊区 需购票
Northwest Suburbs Ticket required

颐和园

Summer Palace

中国现存规模最大、保存最完整的皇家园林，被誉为"皇家园林博物馆"

Largest and best-preserved imperial garden in China, known as the "Imperial Garden Museum"

西北郊 需购票
Northwest Suburbs Ticket required

天坛公园

Temple of Heaven

明清两代皇帝祭天、祈谷的场所，建筑布局和设计蕴含深厚的象征意义

Where emperors of Ming and Qing dynasties worshipped heaven and prayed for good harvests

南部城区 需购票
Southern Urban Area Ticket required

什刹海景区

Shichahai Area

包含前海、后海和西海三个水域，周边有众多历史古迹和胡同，充满老北京风情

Includes three lakes with many historical sites and hutongs nearby, full of old Beijing charm

市中心 免费（部分景点收费）
City Center Free (some attractions charge)

798 Art Zone

Art zone converted from old factories, home to galleries, studios and design companies

旅行实用贴士

Useful Travel Tips

帮助您的北京之旅更加顺利和愉快

Helping make your Beijing trip smoother and more enjoyable

交通出行

Transportation

  • 购买"亿通行"APP或实体交通卡，方便乘坐地铁和公交
  • 地铁是最便捷的出行方式，覆盖大部分景点
  • 使用滴滴出行APP叫车，比路边拦车更方便
  • Get "Yitongxing" app or physical transit card for subway and bus
  • Subway is the most convenient, covering most attractions
  • Use Didi app for taxi service, more convenient than hailing on street

门票预订

Ticket Booking

  • 故宫、长城等热门景点需提前1-7天在线预订
  • 使用景点官方微信公众号或小程序预订最可靠
  • 携带护照原件，很多景点需实名登记入园
  • Book popular attractions 1-7 days in advance online
  • Use official WeChat accounts for most reliable bookings
  • Carry original passport, many sites require real-name registration

餐饮美食

Food & Dining

  • 尝试北京烤鸭、涮羊肉等特色美食
  • 大型商场内有很多适合外国游客的餐厅
  • 下载美团或大众点评APP查找附近美食
  • Try Peking duck, hot pot and other local specialties
  • Large shopping malls have many foreigner-friendly restaurants
  • Use Meituan or Dazhongdianping apps to find nearby food

其他贴士

Other Tips

  • 提前下载好翻译软件，如百度翻译或谷歌翻译
  • 携带充电宝，北京景点较大，需要长时间使用手机
  • 尊重当地文化习俗，遵守景区规定和注意事项
  • Download translation apps like Baidu Translate or Google Translate
  • Bring power bank, as Beijing attractions are large and require phone use
  • Respect local customs and follow attraction rules and guidelines
